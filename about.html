About Rukminihall Jewellers

A legacy of trust, craftsmanship, and purity since 1962

Our Story – Six Decades of Excellence

Founded in 1962 by the late Sri Rukminihallappa, our journey began in the heart of Channarayapatna with a simple vision: to create jewellery that would become family heirlooms. What started as a small workshop has today grown into one of the most trusted names in Indian jewellery.

For over 60 years, we have remained committed to our founding principles of uncompromising quality, ethical sourcing, and exceptional craftsmanship. Each piece that leaves our workshop carries with it generations of knowledge and a promise of purity.

Today, the third generation of the Rukminihall family continues this legacy, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design to create jewellery that speaks to modern sensibilities while honoring our rich heritage.

Master Craftsmanship – The Art of Perfection

Our master artisans, many of whom have been with us for over 30 years, bring centuries-old techniques to every piece they create. From intricate temple jewellery to modern minimalist designs, each creation undergoes rigorous quality checks.

We take pride in our in-house design team that works closely with customers to create bespoke pieces that tell their unique stories. Every gem is hand-selected, every setting carefully crafted, ensuring that when you buy from us, you're investing in perfection.
