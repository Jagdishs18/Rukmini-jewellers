Refund & Returns Policy

Last Updated: March 2025 | Version 1.0

Our Promise: At RUKMINIHALL JEWELLERS, customer satisfaction is our priority. We stand behind the quality of our products and offer a fair return and refund policy.

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

Eligible for Return:

  • Defective/Damaged Products: If you receive a damaged or defective item
  • Wrong Product: If you receive an item different from what you ordered
  • Quality Issues: If the product quality does not match the description
  • Size Issues: If the jewellery size doesn't fit (for rings, bangles, etc.)

Not Eligible for Return:

  • Custom-made jewellery: Pieces made to your specific design
  • Personalized items: Engraved or customized pieces
  • Sale items: Products purchased during clearance sales
  • Worn or altered items: Products that show signs of wear or have been resized
  • Items without original packaging and tags
  • Orders beyond 7 days of delivery

2. Return Timeline

  • Within 24 hours: Report any visible damage or wrong product immediately
  • Within 3 days: Report sizing issues or quality concerns
  • Within 7 days: Return request must be initiated for any eligible reason
  • After 7 days: No returns accepted, but we offer repair services at nominal cost
⚠️ Important: Return requests must be initiated within 7 days of delivery. No exceptions will be made for requests after this period.

3. Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Call or WhatsApp us at +91 98100 12345 within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide Details: Share your order number, photos/videos of the issue
  3. Get Approval: Our team will verify and provide a Return Authorization Number
  4. Pack the Item: Securely pack the item in original packaging with all tags
  5. Ship Back: Courier pickup will be arranged or you can ship to our address
  6. Quality Check: Item undergoes inspection at our facility (2-3 days)
  7. Refund/Replacement: Processed within 5-7 business days after approval

4. Refund Options

Return Reason Refund Method Timeline
Defective/Damaged Product Full refund or replacement 5-7 business days
Wrong Product Delivered Full refund or exchange 5-7 business days
Size Issue Exchange or store credit 5-7 business days
Quality Concerns Subject to inspection 7-10 business days
Change of Mind Not eligible -

5. Refund Methods

  • Original Payment Method: Refund to the same card/UPI/account used for purchase (5-7 business days)
  • Store Credit: Instant credit valid for 1 year (available on request)
  • Bank Transfer: For orders paid by cash on delivery (3-5 business days)
  • Exchange: Replacement with new item of same or higher value (difference to be paid)

6. Cancellation Policy

Order Cancellation by Customer:

  • Within 2 hours of order: Full refund, no questions asked
  • After 2 hours but before dispatch: 90% refund (10% cancellation fee)
  • After dispatch: Cannot be cancelled, must follow return process
  • Custom orders: Cannot be cancelled once production starts

Cancellation by RUKMINIHALL JEWELLERS:

  • Out of stock: Full refund if we cannot fulfill the order
  • Payment issues: Order cancelled if payment fails/not received
  • Suspicious orders: We reserve the right to cancel any order for security reasons

7. Exchange Policy

  • Size Exchange: Free size exchange within 7 days (shipping charges may apply)
  • Design Exchange: Can exchange for another design of equal or higher value
  • Price Difference: If new item is costlier, difference to be paid; if cheaper, store credit issued
  • Exchange Limit: Only one exchange per order

8. Refund Timeline

Stage Timeframe
Return request approval 24-48 hours
Item received at our facility 2-5 days (depends on location)
Quality inspection 2-3 business days
Refund processing 3-5 business days
Money credited to your account 3-7 business days (bank dependent)
Total time (estimated) 10-15 business days

9. Shipping Costs for Returns

  • Defective/Wrong item: We bear all shipping costs
  • Size exchange: Customer pays return shipping; we pay forward shipping
  • Change of mind: Not applicable (returns not accepted)
  • International returns: Customer bears both ways shipping + customs

10. Product Condition for Return

Returned items must be:

  • In original condition (unworn, unaltered)
  • With all original packaging, boxes, and certificates
  • With all tags attached
  • Free from scratches, damage, or wear
  • Not resized or altered in any way
⚠️ Items that don't meet these conditions will be rejected and shipped back at customer's expense.

11. Special Cases

Bridal/Jewellery Sets:

  • Must be returned as complete set (partial returns not accepted)
  • Special inspection required (may take longer)

High-Value Items (above ₹1,00,000):

  • Video verification of packing required
  • Insurance verification may be needed
  • Extended inspection period (5-7 days)

Sale/Discounted Items:

  • Final sale - no returns or exchanges
  • Only eligible if defective

13. Customer Rights

  • You have the right to a refund if the product is defective or not as described
  • You have the right to cancel within 2 hours of order
  • You have the right to a replacement for damaged goods
  • You have the right to be informed about the status of your return

14. Dispute Resolution

In case of any disputes regarding returns or refunds:

  1. First, contact our customer service for resolution
  2. If unresolved, escalation to Store Manager within 48 hours
  3. Final escalation to Management (response within 7 days)
  4. Legal jurisdiction: Courts in Hassan, Karnataka
